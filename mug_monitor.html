Mug Monitor

The advanced companion app for your smart mug. Get reliable background monitoring, persistent notifications, and visual pulsing alerts.

Get it on Google Play

Background Monitoring

Mug Monitor runs a robust background service to keep track of your beverage. Premium users get a persistent silent notification showing live temperature, battery life, and current state.

Reliable Alerts

Our notification system is designed for stability. You'll know exactly when your drink is perfectly hot, even if your phone is in your pocket or the app is closed.

Visual Pulsing

Don't just rely on your phone. Mug Monitor pulses your mug's LED in your personalized color when your drink is ready. See the status at a glance without unlocking your device.

Stay Informed Instantly

Live updates right in your notification shade.
