Bluetooth and Location Data

To ensure the functionality of the application, we require specific permissions related to Bluetooth and Location Services.

"This app uses Bluetooth and Location Services solely to scan for and connect to your Ember Mug. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data to any server."

Why is Location Permission needed?
On the Android operating system, scanning for Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices requires the "Location" permission, as BLE beacons can theoretically be used to determine a user's location. However, our application uses this permission strictly to locate your Bluetooth device nearby and does not use it for GPS tracking or mapping.
